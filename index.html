About

Rental Management App

Version 1.0.0

A simple, offline app to track your monthly rent, WiFi, and electricity bills using the Bikram Sambat calendar.

Features:

  • Fully offline functionality
  • Bikram Sambat date support
  • Color-coded payment status
  • Auto-fill previous month data
  • Export/Import capabilities
  • Mobile-friendly design
